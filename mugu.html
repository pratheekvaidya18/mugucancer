Mugu's Journey

A Story of Courage, Hope, and Recovery

Biography

Name: Mugu
Age: 3 years old
Gender: Female
Father's Name: Mr. Siddesh Dalal
Diagnosis: Brain Tumor (Medulloblastoma-Garde IV subtype-3)
Date of Onset: December 2024

Mugu is a brave 3-year-old girl who has shown incredible resilience in her battle against a brain tumor. Her journey began in December 2024 when she started experiencing symptoms that led to her diagnosis. Despite her young age, Mugu has faced each challenge with remarkable courage, supported by her loving family and dedicated medical team.

December 2024

Initial Symptoms

Mugu began showing symptoms including persistent headaches, vomiting, irritability, and behavioral changes.

December 2024

Diagnosis & Surgery

Medical evaluations and MRI revealed a brain tumor. Brain tumor removal surgery was performed at Jupiter Hospital, Thane.

January 2025

Recovery & Transfer

Post-surgery recovery and transfer to Tata Memorial Cancer Hospital, Mumbai for specialized oncology care.

July 2025

Ongoing Treatment

Currently receiving age-appropriate chemotherapy and regular monitoring with MRI scans and blood tests.
